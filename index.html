A Bit About Me

I'm currently diving into the practical side of things as an Article Trainee at SAR Associates, a firm of Chartered Accountants, since February 2025. Before that, I had a fun run as President of the GCM Social Club from 2022 to 2023. We organized some engaging events like chats on youth economy with folks like MP Gagan Thapa and Economist Dr. YubaRaj Khatiwada, plus a session on socio-economic issues with Ex-Governor Dr. Chiranjibi Nepal and journalist Mr. Rabindra Mishra. We also ran blood donations with the Nepal Red Cross Society, donated to Manav Kalyan Samaj, and pitched in for the Bagmati Cleanliness Program, it was all about giving back and connecting with the community.

On the skills front, I enjoy project management, event planning, and getting my head around tax matters. I've picked up a couple of honors along the way: The Best Student of the Year and a Silver Medal in the National Business Case Analysis.
